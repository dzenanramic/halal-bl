Essential Information

Practical resources for Muslim travelers in Banja Luka

Travel Essentials

Qibla Direction

The Qibla direction in Banja Luka is Southeast (135° from North). Most mosques have indicators, and prayer rooms typically have signs showing the direction.

Learn More

Basic Bosnian Phrases

Useful phrases for Muslim travelers:

  • As-salamu alaykum - Peace be upon you
  • Gdje je džamija? - Where is the mosque?
  • Imate li halal hranu? - Do you have halal food?
  • Hvala - Thank you

Emergency Contacts

Important numbers for travelers:

  • General Emergency: 112
  • Police: 122
  • Ambulance: 124
  • Fire Department: 123
  • Islamic Community: +387 51 432 156
